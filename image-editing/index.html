Image Editing Project

This project introduced two main design tasks in digital image editing: a book cover and a logo design. Through these assignments, I explored bitmap and vector-based techniques, and practiced creating visuals that communicate themes effectively.

Book Cover Design

Echoes of the Forest: The Harmony of Nature and Music

This book cover design combines natural textures and a muted color palette to express the theme of harmony between the forest and music. The visual elements highlight a calm and immersive narrative tone.

Logo Design

Creative Keys

The Creative Keys logo merges the outline of a piano key with a fluid graphic shape to convey creativity, clarity, and musical identity. The design emphasizes simplicity and versatility for use in various digital contexts.
